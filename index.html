Yichong Zhao (Eric, 赵奕冲) is an AI engineer at Preferred Networks, a Tokyo-based AI unicorn, in its AI Products & Solutions division — where he builds LLM-based agents for operations-research problems and, more broadly, for the kind of complex, open-ended problems found in the real world. He also works on the post-training of nucleic-acid sequence language models within a JST CREST project.

Research interests

  • AI agents — for search, exploration, and open-ended real-world tasks.
  • LLM / VLM post-training & alignment — strengthening meta-skills and agentic capability.
  • Accelerating scientific discovery with LLMs — hypothesis generation, experimentation, and reasoning over the literature.
